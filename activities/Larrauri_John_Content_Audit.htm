Basic Information
Site's Main Objective & Description The site's main objective is to provide potential customer's with all the necessary information about the goods and services offered by Top Stone Inc.  This includes all different fountains, sculptures, busts, marble and granite works, etc.  The site should show customers what Top Stone does without overdoing it.  The customer should be encouraged on the site to contact Top Stone or visit the physical store to place orders or with questions.
Home Page<title> Slogan - Top Stone, Inc.  You're # 1 Stop For Rotating Marble Fountains!!, The home page will provide a clear cut navigation link, a scrolling gallery of some of Top Stone's Pieces, Clear company branding
About Page<title> The about page will provide the customer with pertinent information on Top Stone's history and what it does
Catalog Page<title> The catalog page will provide images of some key pieces that Top Stone can supply.  Pieces will be divided into categories and key pieces within each category will be displayed.  Customers will be encouraged to contact Top Stone for orders, special orders, as well as be encouraged to visit the physical location to view pieces.
Contact Page<title> The content page will be a contact form that will be emailed to Top Stone for any customer questions that may arise.
Terms & Conditions Page<title> The terms & conditions page will show the rules about ordering from and dealing with Top Stone so that customer's questions about company ordering policies can be easily answered.
Store Location Page<title> The location page will provide customers with the exact location of the store as well as a link to get directions to it.
